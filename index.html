How do I get a location's coordinates?

On a computer: open Google Maps, right-click the exact spot, and click the latitude/longitude numbers at the top of the menu to copy them (e.g. 40.7128, -74.0060). Paste the first number into Latitude and the second into Longitude. On your phone: tap and hold the spot to drop a pin, then copy the coordinates that appear.
